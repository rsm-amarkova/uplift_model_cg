Uplift Modeling for Targeted Marketing

Comparing propensity modeling and uplift modeling to maximize incremental profit.

Business Objective

Traditional response models optimize for likelihood of conversion. This project evaluates whether uplift modeling can more effectively identify customers whose behavior changes because of treatment — improving targeting efficiency and maximizing incremental profit.

Approach

  • Constructed treatment and control datasets
  • Built baseline propensity model
  • Implemented uplift modeling framework
  • Evaluated using AUC and profit-based performance metrics

Impact & Evaluation

Performance was evaluated not only on predictive accuracy but on business-relevant metrics including incremental profit generation.
